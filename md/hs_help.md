# FAQ

## 🔐 Account & Security
- **Q: What should I do if I forget my password?**
  > You can reset your password on the login screen. Click the lock icon, and the system will ask you to authenticate as the device owner (using fingerprint or face recognition).
  > If you want to disable this feature completely, you can turn off "Allow password recovery" in the settings.

- **Q: What are "Camouflage App" and "Fake Mode"?**
  > You can select "Camouflage App" in settings to change the app icon to a calculator or other common tool, making it completely "invisible" on your home screen.
  > With "Fake Mode" enabled, you can set up a fake gesture/PIN. When you log in with these fake credentials, the app enters a completely different, empty, or non-sensitive fake space, used to handle situations where others force you to show the app.
  
- **Q: Where are my private files stored?**
  > All hidden files are safely stored in the `.pg` folder within your device's internal storage. Do not manually delete this folder, as it will cause data loss.

## 📸 Media Management
- **Q: How to quickly hide photos or videos?**
  > You can select files in your system gallery, tap "Share", and then select "Hide Something" to hide them in seconds.

- **Q: Which formats are supported?**
  > We support almost all mainstream image and video formats, including 4K Ultra HD video.

- **Q: What are Auto-Clips and Shuffle Play?**
  > **Auto-Clips** automatically captures highlights from long videos for random display; **Shuffle Play** provides a random playback experience, keeping your private cinema moments fresh.

## 🌐 Browser & Cross-platform
- **Q: Is the private browser safe?**
  > Our built-in browser data, including bookmarks and browsing history, stays entirely within the app and is invisible to the outside world, providing a one-stop "search-browse-download-hide" closed-loop experience.

- **Q: How to access files on my phone from a computer?**
  > After enabling "PC/Mac Access", the app starts a temporary HTTP server within the local network. Just enter the displayed address in your computer's browser to safely manage files, no cloud sync required, zero leak risk.
